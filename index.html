Me, Myself, and I

My name is DAVID ODHIAMBO. I recently completed my Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from Kisii University. I have a passion for technology and everything else that exists on the internet. In addition to web design and development, I am also an enthusiast of cybersecurity and currently taking an online short course on the same, along with ethical hacking. I am also a holder of the Google IT Technical Support Professional course certificate from Coursera (Online course).

One thing I love about IT and its related fields is the problem-solving aspect. It is a career that allows you to exercise your creativity and come up with solutions to users' issues. You can learn and practice an array of different skills and eventually determine where you want to focus your career. Just like in real life, taking failure as feedback is a skill in IT that only better equips you to tackle them next time around.

"We live, We learn, We level up, one day at a time."
